D3 Axes

Now that we have x and y scales we can use those to draw axes. D3 takes care of the lines, groups and text and adds ticks in the right places.

var xAxis = d3.svg.axis()
  .scale(x)
  .orient("bottom")
  .ticks(21);

var yAxis = d3.svg.axis()
  .orient("left");

svg.append("g")
  .attr("class", "x axis")
  .attr("transform", "translate(0," + (height) + ")")
  .call(xAxis);

svg.append("g")
  .attr("class", "y axis");

Notice that since we haven't finished our y scale yet so we can't scale the y axis.
