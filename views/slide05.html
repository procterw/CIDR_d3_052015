D3 Basics: SVG Shapes

The core of a D3 visualization is the manipulation of the various SVG shapes:circle, ellipse, line, polygon, polyline and rect.

<circle cx="25" cy="25" r="25" />

<ellipse cx="50" cy="25" rx="50" ry="25" />

<line x1="0" x2="50" y1="0" y2="50" />

<polygon points="30,0 50,12 50,32 30,45 10,32 10,12" />

<polyline points="30,0 50,12 50,32 30,45 10,32" fill="none" />

<rect width="50" height="50" x="0" y="0" />
