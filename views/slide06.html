D3 Basics: Other Essential SVG Elements

text and g (group) are almost always present in D3 visualizations. g is used to group elements together. A group can be transformed as one unit. text is what you'd expect.

<g transform="rotate(45)">
  <text x="50" y="-20">Thing1</text>
  <text x="50" y="0">Thing1</text>
  <text x="50" y="20">Thing1</text>
</g>
